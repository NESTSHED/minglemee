Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

18+ Platform

Mingle Mee is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. Users under 18 are not permitted to use the application.

Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Sexualized content involving minors
  • Any activity that endangers children

Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate content and users directly within the app.

Violations may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Account suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to law enforcement when required

Compliance

Mingle Mee complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with relevant authorities regarding reports of child exploitation and abuse.
