Mingle Mee Account Deletion Information

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Mingle Mee app.
  2. Go to the Profile page.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your decision.

Once completed, your account will be permanently deleted.

Data Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Profile image
  • Authentication account

Data That May Be Retained

To help prevent abuse, fraud, and violations of our policies, certain records may be retained for safety and moderation purposes, including:

  • Block records
  • Reports and moderation records
  • Account deletion logs
